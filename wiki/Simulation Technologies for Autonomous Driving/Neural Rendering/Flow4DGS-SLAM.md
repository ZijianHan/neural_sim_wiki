# Flow4DGS-SLAM

## Definition
Flow4DGS-SLAM is an optical-flow-guided dynamic 4D Gaussian Splatting SLAM framework for joint tracking and dynamic-scene reconstruction.

## Synthesis
### Source-grounded Highlights
- Camera-induced motion decomposition for category-agnostic dynamic masking and better pose initialization.
- Hybrid 4D Gaussian modeling for dynamic elements.
- Claimed gains in tracking, dynamic reconstruction quality, and efficiency.

### 中文版本
Flow4DGS-SLAM 强调将光流先验与动态 4D 高斯建图结合，以提升动态场景中的跟踪与重建效率。
